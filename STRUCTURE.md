## How to Navigate

**If you're designing AI-powered content:**
Start with [AI Content Strategy](templates/ai-content/ai-content-strategy.md), then explore [Ethical AI Guidelines](resources/ethical-ai/ethical-ai-guidelines.md)

**If you're building conversational interfaces:**
Check out [Conversation Flows](templates/conversational/conversation-flows.md) and [Prompt Design](templates/ai-content/prompt-design.md)

**If you're measuring content effectiveness:**
Browse [Testing & Measurement workflows](workflows/testing/) for frameworks and methods

**If you're writing core content:**
Browse [templates](templates/) by content type (messages, flows, documentation)

**If you're establishing processes:**
Review [workflows](workflows/) for Git, review, and testing processes

**If you're ensuring ethical AI:**
Explore [ethical AI resources](resources/ethical-ai/) for bias detection, safety, and transparency

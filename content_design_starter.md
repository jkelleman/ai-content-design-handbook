## üó£Ô∏è Voice and Tone Guidelines

### Product Voice
How should the product always sound?

- [ ] **Professional** - Knowledgeable and trustworthy
- [ ] **Friendly** - Approachable and warm
- [ ] **Direct** - Clear and to-the-point
- [ ] **Playful** - Fun and energetic
- [ ] **Empowering** - Confident and supportive

*Choose 2-3 that fit your product*

### Tone by Context

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| First time use | Encouraging, clear | "Let's get started! This will take about 2 minutes." |
| Error state | Helpful, calm | "Connection failed. Check your internet and try again." |
| Success | Positive, brief | "Dataset uploaded!" |
| Empty state | Motivating, informative | "No pipelines yet. Create one to get started." |

### Uncertainty Handling
How will the AI express confidence/uncertainty?

- **High confidence:** 
  *Example: "Based on your data, sales increased 23% in Q4."*

- **Medium confidence:** 
  *Example: "It looks like sales increased, but I'd recommend verifying the exact percentage."*

  *Example: "I'm not sure about that. Would you like me to connect you with support?"*

*Example: "I didn't understand that. Could you rephrase your question? Or type 'help' to see what I can do."*

*Example: Offer human support when AI fails 2+ times or user explicitly requests it*

## ‚ôø Accessibility Checklist

- [ ] **Plain language:** Written at 8th grade reading level or lower
- [ ] **Alt text:** All images have descriptive alt text
- [ ] **Link text:** Links describe destination ("View pricing" not "Click here")
- [ ] **Headings:** Proper heading hierarchy (H1 ‚Üí H2 ‚Üí H3)
- [ ] **Error messages:** Clearly state problem and solution
- [ ] **Color:** Don't rely on color alone to convey meaning
- [ ] **Abbreviations:** Spelled out on first use
- [ ] **Jargon:** Technical terms explained or avoided

## üõ°Ô∏è Ethical AI Checklist

*Complete if your feature uses AI*

- [ ] Users know they're interacting with AI

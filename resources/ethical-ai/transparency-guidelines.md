# Transparency Guidelines for AI Content

A framework for honest, clear disclosure about AI use in content experiences.

## Why Transparency Matters

**Users deserve to know when they're interacting with AI.**

**Benefits of transparency:**
- **Builds trust** - Honesty creates credibility
- **Sets expectations** - Users know what AI can/can't do
- **Enables informed consent** - Users choose to engage
- **Legal compliance** - Many regulations require disclosure
- **Reduces harm** - Clear limitations prevent over-reliance

**Costs of opacity:**
- **Erodes trust** - Users feel deceived when they discover AI
- **Increases liability** - Hidden AI use creates legal risk
- **Damages brand** - Perceived as dishonest or manipulative
- **Disappoints users** - Unclear capabilities lead to frustration

### 5. **Admit Uncertainty**

**AI should acknowledge when it doesn't know something.**

**Don't fake confidence:**

```markdown
âŒ Bad (Confident but wrong):

âœ… Good (Honest about uncertainty):

Let me check the documentation."
[Retrieves accurate info]

Or: "I don't have that specific information. 
Check the documentation or contact support for the exact limit."
```

**Confidence indicators:**

```markdown
High confidence:
"According to the documentation, [fact]."

Medium confidence:
"Based on what I know, [info], but you should verify this."

Low confidence:
"I'm not certain, but you might want to check [source]."

No confidence:
"I don't know. For accurate information, please [alternative]."
```

**When to say "I don't know":**

```markdown
Say "I don't know" when:
- Information not in training data
- Question outside scope
- Confidence below threshold
- Request requires judgment
- Ambiguous or unclear query

Provide:
- Alternative resources
- Human contact option
- How to find answer
- Related information you do know
```

## Key Takeaways

1. **Always disclose AI use** - Users have a right to know
2. **Be honest about limitations** - Don't overpromise
3. **Provide user control** - Let users opt out and manage data
4. **Make it understandable** - Plain language, not jargon
5. **Test comprehension** - Ensure users actually understand
6. **Stay compliant** - Know your legal requirements
7. **Iterate** - Transparency practices evolve with AI

---

**Remember:** Transparency is not just about legal compliance. It's about respecting users and building trust. Honest disclosure about AI use, capabilities, and limitations creates better relationships and better products.
